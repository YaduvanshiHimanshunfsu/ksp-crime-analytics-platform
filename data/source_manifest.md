## How to Import Public Data

1. Download CSV from Karnataka OGD or Kaggle
2. Place it in `data/raw/`
3. Run: `python execution/import_public_aggregate.py --input data/raw/<filename>.csv`
4. Or generate sample data: `python execution/import_public_aggregate.py --generate-sample`
